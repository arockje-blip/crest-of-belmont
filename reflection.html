The Journey Unfolds

Self Reflection

Belmont's Clan | Arock Jeberson J

Hi, I am Arock Jeberson J from the Belmont's Clan. When I first stepped into this community, I was a complete novice. I didn't know anything about coding or the vast digital world related to it. The turning point came when Michal Nithish Bro stepped in as a guide. He helped me navigate through the noise and choose a path that truly resonated with me—I chose AI/ML.

Since that decision, my growth has been exponential. I started studying Python from the ground up, completing Python 2 and Python 3, and I have already mastered about 30% of Advanced Python. But it wasn't just about the logic; it was also about the structure. I dive into HTML, leveraging my previous experience in creating websites to build more sophisticated digital spaces.

The support from the community has been unwavering. Michal Nithish Bro helped me a lot by supporting my technical journey, but I also learned practical life skills from our community tasks. I now know how to repair laptops, computers, and PCs—skills that extend far beyond a screen. Completing the CS50 Course provided me with a foundational logic that I use every day, especially as I delve into Linux and learn how to use Python to automate tasks and streamline my workflow.

Our Weekly Bashes were more than just meetings; they were workshops of the mind. I learned about Scratch Programming and understood the core concept of how a true technical community works. The energy was always high, thanks to Hamdan Bro for constantly cheering us on and Falin Bro and Maxwell Rubert Bro for being the bashers who inspired me to join the Byte Bash Blitz community. Along the way, I’ve gained more than just knowledge—I’ve gained a new circle of friends who push me to be better.

Of course, the journey had its hurdles. I attempted to use my new knowledge to build a physical Model, but it didn't work properly at first. I realized that a single power supply wasn't enough to run the complexity I was aiming for. This failure taught me more about hardware limitations and engineering than success ever could. Finally, my web development journey came full circle as Geo Nithin Bro helped me understand how to truly deploy and host a website for the world to see.

Looking back, I am no longer that person who "doesn't know anything about coding." I am a builder, a learner, and a proud member of the Belmont's Clan, ready for the next challenge.

I would also like to express my deepest appreciation to our former CB Sriharshini, CBS Aadhithya Mahesh, and Geo Nithin for their incredible support and guidance. Their leadership helped our clan attain the peak of success. A special thanks to all our Leaders and Members for their constant presence and collaboration.

To every Rookie: you are the heroes who provided the strong competition that pushed me to grow. Each challenge you presented was a reason for my improvement, and I am grateful to be part of such a talented group.

Special thanks to my mentors Michal Nithish Bro, Hamdan Bro, Falin Bro, Geo Nithin Bro, and Aadhithya Mahesh for their invaluable guidance throughout this journey.
